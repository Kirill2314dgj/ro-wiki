How to Make a Game Pass on Roblox

𝕏 Twitter 📱 Telegram 📘 VK
📅 March 10, 2026
👤 By RO-Wiki Team
⏱️ 8 min read

Game passes are one of the best ways to monetize your Roblox experience. This guide walks you through the entire process — from opening the Creator Hub to setting a price and grabbing your Pass ID for scripts.

Overview

In this tutorial, you'll learn how to create a game pass, customize it with a name and thumbnail, enable sales, and set a price in Robux. We'll also show you where to find your Pass ID so you can use it in your game scripts later.

What you'll need

  • A Roblox account with publishing permissions
  • A published experience in your Creator Dashboard
  • An image for your game pass thumbnail (512x512 pixels works best)
1

Open Roblox

Start by launching Roblox. You can do this either through the Roblox Player app on your computer, or by visiting the Roblox website in your browser. Make sure you're logged into your account.

🎮 Roblox App/Website
2

Go to Create

Once you're logged in, navigate to the Creator Hub. On the website, click the "Create" button in the top navigation bar.

🎨 Creator Hub
3

Select your game

In the Creator Hub, find your game under "Experiences" and click on its title.

📋 Your Games List
4

Go to Passes

In the game management panel, go to Monetization → Passes.

💰 Monetization → Passes
5

Click "Create A Pass"

On the Passes page, click the Create A Pass button.

➕ Create A Pass
6

Customize your pass

Fill in:

  • Name: Clear and appealing
  • Description: What the buyer gets
  • Image: 512x512 pixel image

Click Save Changes.

7

Enable sales

Go to the Sales tab and toggle Item for Sale to on.

💡 Your pass won't be visible until you enable this!
8

Set a price

Enter the price in Robux. You earn 70% of each sale. Click Save Changes.

💰 Check similar games for competitive pricing!

Using the Pass ID in a Script

After you have your Pass ID, use this script in ServerScriptService:

local MarketplaceService = game:GetService("MarketplaceService")
local PASS_ID = 123456789  -- Replace with your ID

game.Players.PlayerAdded:Connect(function(player)
    local success, ownsPass = pcall(function()
        return MarketplaceService:UserOwnsGamePassAsync(player.UserId, PASS_ID)
    end)

    if success and ownsPass then
        print(player.Name .. " owns the pass! Grant benefits.")
        -- Add your benefits here
    end
end)

Frequently Asked Questions

How much Robux do I earn?

You earn 70% of the price. For a 100 Robux pass, you receive 70 Robux.

Can I change the price later?

Yes, anytime from the Sales tab.

Do I need an image?

Recommended, but Roblox provides a default placeholder.
