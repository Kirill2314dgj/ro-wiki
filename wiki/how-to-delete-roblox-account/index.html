How to Delete Your Roblox Account

𝕏 Twitter 📱 Telegram 📘 VK
March 10, 2026
By Celsos Ricablanca
3 min read

Whether you're taking a break from Roblox or want to permanently remove your presence from the platform, deleting your account isn't as simple as clicking a button. Here's exactly how to do it.

⚠️ Think carefully before deleting

This action is permanent and irreversible. Once your account is deleted:

  • All game progress, items, Robux, and purchases are lost forever
  • Your username becomes available for others to use
  • You cannot recover any data or transfer it to a new account
  • If you want to play Roblox again, you must start from scratch

Consider temporary deactivation or just taking a break instead of permanent deletion.

1 Visit Roblox Support

Open your browser and go to the Roblox Support form. Make sure you're logged into the account you want to delete before proceeding.

[Screenshot: Roblox Support form]
2 Provide Contact Information

Fill in your email address. You'll need to verify it twice – Roblox wants to be absolutely sure it's you. Your username and first name are optional but recommended.

[Screenshot: Contact information form]
3 Select Issue Type

Under "Type of help", select "Delete My Account" from the dropdown menu. You'll be prompted to confirm this selection twice.

Alternative method: You can also select "Data Privacy Request" → "Right to be Forgotten (RTBF)" which achieves the same result.
4 Specify Your Request

In the "Description of issue" field, clearly state that you want to permanently delete your Roblox account. You can add a brief reason if you want, but it's not required.

Example message: "I would like to permanently delete my Roblox account. Please process this request and confirm once completed."
[Screenshot: Issue description field]
5 Submit and Wait

Click "Submit" and wait for Roblox Customer Support to respond. This usually takes 1-3 business days. They'll send you an email with:

  • Confirmation that they received your request
  • Additional verification steps (if needed)
  • Final confirmation when your account is deleted
[Screenshot: Submitted form confirmation]

🔄 Alternative: Data Privacy Request Method

Some users prefer the privacy request route. Here's how:

  1. In the Support form, select "Data Privacy Request" as the help type
  2. Choose "Right to be Forgotten (RTBF)" from the sub-options
  3. Complete the same verification process
  4. Submit and wait for confirmation

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long does account deletion take?

Once Roblox Support confirms your request, deletion is usually processed within 7-14 days. They'll email you when it's complete.

Can I cancel a deletion request?

Yes, but only before it's processed. If you change your mind, reply to the confirmation email immediately and ask to cancel the request.

What happens to my Robux and items?

Everything is permanently lost – Robux, game passes, limited items, and all progress. There is no refund or transfer option.

Will my username become available?

Yes, after deletion, your username returns to the pool and can be claimed by anyone.

Can I just deactivate my account instead?

Roblox doesn't offer a deactivation feature – it's either active or permanently deleted. If you're unsure, just stop playing and keep your account.
