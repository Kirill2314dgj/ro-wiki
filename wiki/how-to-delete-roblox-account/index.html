How to Make a Game Pass on Roblox (8 Easy Steps)

𝕏 Twitter 📱 Telegram 📘 VK
📅 March 10, 2026
👤 By RO-Wiki Team
⏱️ 8 min read

Game passes are one of the best ways to monetize your Roblox experience. This guide walks you through the entire process — from opening the Creator Hub to setting a price and grabbing your Pass ID for scripts.

Overview

In this tutorial, you'll learn how to create a game pass, customize it with a name and thumbnail, enable sales, and set a price in Robux. We'll also show you where to find your Pass ID so you can use it in your game scripts later.

What you'll need

  • A Roblox account with publishing permissions.
  • A published (or saved) experience in your Creator Dashboard.
  • An image for your game pass thumbnail (512x512 pixels works best).
1

Open Roblox

Start by launching Roblox. You can do this either through the Roblox Player app on your computer, or by visiting the Roblox website in your browser. Make sure you're logged into your account.

[Screenshot: Roblox homepage]
🎮 Roblox App/Website
2

Go to Create

Once you're logged in, navigate to the Creator Hub (also called the Create page). On the website, click the "Create" button usually located in the top navigation bar.

[Screenshot: Create button]
🎨 Creator Hub
3

Select your game

In the Creator Hub, you'll see a list of your experiences under the "Experiences" tab. Find the game you want to create a pass for and click on its title.

[Screenshot: Experiences list]
📋 Your Games List
4

Go to Passes

Inside your game's management panel, look for the "Monetization" section in the left-hand menu. Click on it, and then select "Passes".

[Screenshot: Monetization menu]
💰 Monetization → Passes
5

Click "Create A Pass"

On the Passes page, make sure you're on the Creations tab. Click the blue button that says "Create A Pass" to get started.

[Screenshot: Create A Pass button]
➕ Create A Pass Button
6

Customize your game pass

Fill in the details:

  • Name: Make it clear and appealing
  • Description: Explain what the buyer gets
  • Image: Upload a 512x512 pixel image

Click "Save Changes" to create the pass.

7

Enable sales

Find your new pass in the list and click on it. Go to the "Sales" tab and toggle "Item for Sale" to on.

💡 Your pass won't be visible for purchase until you enable this!
8

Set a price

Enter the amount of Robux you want to charge. You earn 70% of each transaction. Click "Save Changes".

💰 Pro tip: Check similar games to see competitive pricing!

Using the Pass ID in a Script

After you have your Pass ID, use this script in ServerScriptService:

local MarketplaceService = game:GetService("MarketplaceService")
local PASS_ID = 123456789  -- Replace with your ID

game.Players.PlayerAdded:Connect(function(player)
    local success, ownsPass = pcall(function()
        return MarketplaceService:UserOwnsGamePassAsync(player.UserId, PASS_ID)
    end)

    if success and ownsPass then
        print(player.Name .. " owns the pass! Grant benefits.")
        -- Add your benefits here
    end
end)

Frequently Asked Questions

How much Robux do I earn?

You earn 70% of the Robux price you set. For example, if you sell a pass for 100 Robux, you receive 70 Robux.

Can I change the price later?

Yes, you can change the price anytime from the Sales tab.

Do I need an image?

An image is highly recommended, but Roblox provides a default placeholder if you skip this step.
