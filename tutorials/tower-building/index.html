Tower Building Fundamentals

Design towers that feel fair, challenging, and replayable.

Core rules

  • Increase difficulty gradually every 2–3 sections.
  • Mix timing, precision, and movement variety.
  • Avoid impossible camera angles.

Retention ideas

Add checkpoints for longer towers, clear visual themes, and short reward moments after hard sections.
